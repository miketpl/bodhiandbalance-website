Questions

Pricing FAQ

Do you offer discounts for first-time clients?

Yes! First-time clients receive a complimentary 15-minute consultation. Book your initial session to experience our approach and discuss which package suits you best.

Can I combine different treatments in packages?

Absolutely. Our membership packages allow you to mix and match any of our therapies. Work with our team to create a personalized wellness plan that evolves with your needs.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Monthly membership can be paused or cancelled anytime with no penalties. Gift vouchers do not expire and can be transferred as gifts.

Do you accept insurance or FSA/HSA?

Some clients' insurance plans cover complementary therapies. We can provide invoices for your insurance claim. Please verify your coverage beforehand.

How do I get started with a membership?

Simply book your first session and let our team know you're interested in a membership package. We'll discuss your wellness goals and enroll you on the perfect plan for your journey.

Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there parking available at your location?

+

Yes, we have limited parking available on-site. However, we recommend using the nearby pay-and-display car parks on Great Ormond Street and Taviton Street. Alternatively, our location is easily accessible by public transport — the nearest tube station is Russell Square (Piccadilly Line), just a 5-minute walk away.

Is your space accessible for people with mobility issues?

+

Our treatment room is fully accessible with disabled access on the ground floor, an accessible lift, and an accessible restroom. If you have specific accessibility requirements, please contact us in advance so we can ensure we provide the best experience for you.

What should I wear to my appointment?

+

Wear comfortable, loose clothing that is easy to remove if needed, particularly for reflexology treatments. For most treatments, you may remain clothed, but for full-body therapies like hot stone or full-body massage, you'll disrobe and be provided with comfortable robes and blankets. We'll always explain the process during your consultation.

What can I expect during my first visit?

+

Your first appointment includes a consultation where we discuss your health history, current concerns, and wellness goals. This helps us tailor your treatment to your specific needs. You'll then begin your chosen therapy in a calm, peaceful environment. Most first-time clients report feeling deeply relaxed and rejuvenated after their session.

Do you offer discounts for multiple sessions?

+

Yes, we offer package pricing for multiple sessions. Our wellness membership plans provide significant savings for regular clients. Visit our Pricing page or contact us directly to discuss which package best suits your wellness journey.
